Hello my Name Is Carlos, I will tell you a little about my favorite car brand: Porsche, Porsche usually abbreviated as Porsche AG, is a German car manufacturer specialized in high-end luxury sports cars, without a doubt it is the best world car brand, it meets all expectations from interior to exterior, as well as the engine, one of the best structures of a luxury car.

Porsche has established itself as the greatest exponent of luxury, recognized for its quality, in addition to the manufacturing of sports cars. Now, faced with the challenge of an increasingly ecological society, Porsche is entering the field of sustainability through the manufacture of its first electric vehicle, the Taycan model, which represents another step within this new market, in in tune with new demands.
